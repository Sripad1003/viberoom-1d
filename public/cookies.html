Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help websites remember certain information between sessions.

2. How VibeRoom Uses Cookies

  • Session management: Keep you connected in your current room.
  • User preferences: Save your name and volume preferences (if available).

3. Control Cookies

You can disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may impact functionality.

4. No Third-Party Cookies

We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking.
