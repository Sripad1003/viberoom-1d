About Us

Experience music together, no matter where you are

VibeRoom was born from a simple idea:
Music is better when shared. We wanted to create a space where friends could listen to music together, even when physically apart.

Whether you're across the room or across the world, VibeRoom lets you sync up your music experience with friends and family. Share your favorite tracks, discover new music together, and feel connected through the universal language of music.

Our platform is designed to be intuitive, responsive, and fun - bringing the joy of a shared listening experience to everyone, everywhere.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is VibeRoom free to use?

Yes, VibeRoom is completely free to use. We believe in making music sharing accessible to everyone.

How many people can join a room?

Currently, each room can accommodate up to 10 people simultaneously for the best experience.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed! Simply enter your name and a room code to get started immediately.

What music sources are supported?

Currently, VibeRoom supports YouTube as its primary music source, giving you access to millions of songs.

Is my data private?

We don't store any personal data beyond what's needed for the session. Room data is temporary and deleted when inactive.
